ABOUT

It all began in SURAT

Surat is a vibrant city located in the state of Gujarat, India. It is known as the "Diamond City" due to its significant diamond cutting and polishing industry. Surat is renowned for its textile and diamond businesses, contributing to its status as a major commercial center. The city is situated on the banks of the Tapi River and is known for its rich cultural heritage, including historical landmarks, museums, and festivals. Surat is also recognized for its delectable cuisine, with a wide range of street food and traditional Gujarati dishes to savor.
